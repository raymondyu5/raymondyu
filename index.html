Bio

Hey there! I am a third year undergraduate studying computer science at the Allen School in the University of Washington, Seattle. I am fortunate to be advised by Abhishek Gupta in the WEIRD Lab. My research interests are in robotics, reinforcement learning, and computer vision. As of recently, I have been working on bridging the sim2real gap and open sourcing VLA models. I am also a part-time student researcher on the PRIOR team at the Allen Institute for AI where I work on computer vision based projects.

Outside of work, I enjoy swimming, going on mindless grocery runs, and also trying out new food. Feel free to contact me, let's chat!
